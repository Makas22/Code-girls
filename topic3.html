Top cities in Europe


This blog post is a little different. Welcome friends. Last month we asked you on our Instagram, rather cryptically, what your favourite cities to visit in Europe were and why? We received thousands of responses (thanks!) and this helped us to create a list of the top cities to visit in Europe! So we guess, this blog post is a collab between us and our amazing readers. Heres what you all chose and why:

  1. Rome, Italy: Great food, beautiful architecture, shopping Your favourite tourist attraction: Trevi Fountain.

  2. Munich, Germany: Affordable, beautiful scenery, beer Your favourite tourist attraction: Oktoberfest.

  3. Istanbul, Turkey: Golden hour, rich culture, cosmetic industry Your favourite tourist attraction: Blue Mosque.
