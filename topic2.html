Recommendations for Newlyweds


The wedding bells have rung. The people have danced. The wedding cake has been mercilessly devoured and now it’s time to hop on a plane to begin enjoying the first day of your new forever. Where do you go? Not too sure? Thats where we come in. We know how stressful weddings can be. Bringing all your loved ones together to celebrate the union of two people crazily in love is no small feat. Believe it or not, a lot of couples get so wrapped up In the wedding planning and the actual day, that they forget to think about the next very important thing. The Honeymoon. This is why Meyija travels have curated a simple but informative list for confused couples who need a push In a good direction. You will be spoilt for choice but we guarantee your perfect vacation destination is in this list:

  1. The Maldives: This list wouldn’t be right without starting with the most popular honeymoon destination. You’ve heard about it on tiktok, seen it on instagram. You probably know a friend of a friend who has honeymooned there too. But it’s so popular because this place is truly breathtaking. Home to the most luxurious resorts, sandy beaches, crystalline blue waters and top cuisine, The Maldives should most defintely be a consideration.

  2. Paris, France:: They don’t call it the city of love for no reason. Paris is romance. Imagine a beautiful dinner in the Eiffel Tower, a beautiful boat ride down the canal, or a romantic stroll through the streets of Paris. There’s no doubt you will feel all the love in this beautiful city. I can guarantee you will witness at least one proposal. Prepare the tissues, this one will be a real tear jerker.

  3. Santiago, Chile: A beautiful city with the creme de la creme of art galleries, museums and handicraft markets which blend seamlessly with lively Latino nightlife. You’ll stroll through vibrant neighbourhoods, pop into charming cafes and cute little restaurants, making memories with the love of your life.

  4. Dubai, U.A.E: Dubai is one of the most glamorous destinations you’ll be fortunate enough to visit and with good reason. This is a place where you and your beau can go to be the main characters in your story. Prepare to shop till you drop, to eat some serious food and be treated like serious royalty. You won’t want to leave!
