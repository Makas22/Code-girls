Top 5 countries to visit in the summer

If you’re anything like us at Meyija travels, you probably have one too many countries on your travel bucket list! But how can we blame ourselves. After coming out of a lockdown that seemed like forever and navigating a world post-covid without restrictions, it’s no wonder travel lovers started thinking big again when it comes to their next vacay. Now we know you’re probably going to get carried away when it comes to picking travel destinations. From Europe to the Caribbean, the Middle East or the Americas. We are most certainly spoilt for choice! But Meyija travels have come together to make sure that you don’t miss out on the top 5 countries to visit.

  1. 5: Rio de Janeiro, Brazil: Starting at Number 5 on the list is Brazil. "Rio de Janeiro has always been an iconic city in the world. Home to rich culture, beautiful landscapes and instantly recognisable landmarks, Brazil is a place you go to have maximum enjoyment. Make sure you try to catch the Rio Carnival.

  2. 4: Japan: When someone said visiting Tokyo is like visiting the future they were not lying. At the peak of technology and everything modern is Tokyo. Get lost in the flashing lights and the hustle and bustle of the busy streets. And one thing you MUST have on your to-do list is the Mario Street Go Karting.

  3. 3: Bali, Indonesia: If you haven’t got this destination on your list yet, you most certainly need to. Beautiful beaches, scenery and wildlife, Bali is a must. The locals are so friendly with a great desire to share their culture, from their authentic food to their music. Bar the costly flight to this amazing haven, this holiday is good value.

  4. 2: Cape Town, South Africa: One word: vibes. South Africa is definitely the place to be in summer. The culture is like no other in the world but if you are a music lover, South Africa is not a destination you want to miss. Parties go well into the early hours of the morning, with the sounds of Amapiano coursing through the streets.

  5. 1: Bridgetown Barbados: Yes, at the number one spot is none other than Barbados. It’s the food. It’s the culture. It’s the people. Barbados is a place you will instantly fall in love with. This beautiful island in the Caribbean is well known for having some of the most beautiful beaches in the world. The crystal blue aquamarine waters and soft silky sand put this holiday destination at the very top of all of our lists.
